About Hackathon

IBM(ICE) is organizing a National Level Hackathon on 15th and 16th March 2024 in collaboration with GLA University, Mathura where students from all the IBM ICE Universities can participate in the Hackathon.

This event covers all areas that needs reformation from young minds that will help them towards problem solving and team management skills.This hackathon will focus on the Specializations:

  • Data Analytics
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Cyber Security
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Cloud Computing
